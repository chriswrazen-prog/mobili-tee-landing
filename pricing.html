HSA / FSA Reimbursement

Pre-tax dollars on every tier

Mobili-Tee partners with Truemed to enable HSA/FSA reimbursement for eligible members. A two-minute health survey reviewed by a licensed clinician issues a Letter of Medical Necessity, allowing you to apply pre-tax dollars to your sessions. Typical savings: about 30% versus paying after tax. Fully IRS-compliant. Learn more.
