Introducing Mobili-Tee

What is Mobili-Tee?

Mobili-Tee delivers one-on-one assisted stretching at your club. A certified specialist guides you through a personalized series of stretches on a purpose-built table - releasing tension, restoring range of motion, and supporting the way you already move through your day.

Unlike a static class or a self-guided routine, assisted stretching is a hands-on practice. Your specialist applies precise, controlled pressure to lengthen the muscles and decompress the joints, working through patterns tailored to your body.

It is a quiet, focused twenty-five minutes that meaningfully changes how the rest of your day - and your next round - feels.

Our Story

Built at the intersection of mobility and membership

Mobili-Tee began with a simple observation: the members most invested in golf, tennis, and pickleball don’t lack motivation - they lack accessible, high-quality recovery. The closest stretch studios are a drive across town. The home routine never quite happens. And the thing that would actually keep them on the course, on the court, and in the gym - focused, hands-on mobility work - has rarely lived where members already are.

When the assisted-stretch studio many of us had quietly built our routines around announced it would close in May 2026, the gap became impossible to ignore. Mobili-Tee is our answer.

Mobili-Tee places certified assisted-stretch specialists inside private clubs, working from purpose-built tables in quiet, dedicated spaces. Sessions are short, structured, and personal: a mobility assessment on the first visit, a custom protocol on every visit after. Members book and pay through our own platform - fully independent of the club, with no operational lift on staff or systems.

The result is recovery that doesn’t compete with the experience your members value - it extends it.

A Note from the Founder

Why we’re starting inside private clubs

I came to Mobili-Tee the long way around. My career has been in fixed income markets - a world where most days demand stillness, focus, and far too many hours in a chair. My antidote, for as long as I can remember, has been golf.

I joined White Manor in 2017, stepped away from the club in 2018 for an expat assignment in London, and rejoined private club life on my repatriation. I’ve spent the years since rebuilding a routine around the rounds, the tennis, and the company you can only really find at a private club - and I’ve come to believe these clubs deserve a recovery experience that matches the rest of what they offer.

Like a lot of members, I’d quietly been relying on a local assisted-stretch studio for years - the single most effective thing I’d ever done for my golf and for my body. When I learned that studio would be closing its doors in May 2026, I realized two things at once: the work mattered too much to lose, and the place it most belonged was inside the clubs where members already spent their best hours.

Mobili-Tee launches this summer at Radley Run Country Club - our first home, with additional clubs to follow. A certified specialist, a quiet room, a table built for the work, and a focused window that genuinely changes how the next round, and the rest of the week, feels.

If you’re a member, club leadership, or simply curious, I’d be glad to keep you informed.

- Chris Wrazen, Founder, Mobili-Tee

Frequently Asked

Common questions

What is assisted stretching?

Assisted stretching is a guided, hands-on mobility practice. A trained specialist applies precise, controlled pressure to lengthen muscles and decompress joints in patterns the body cannot easily reproduce on its own.

How long is a session?

Sessions are 25 or 50 minutes on a purpose-built stretch table. Your first visit includes a brief mobility assessment.

Where will Mobili-Tee be available?

Mobili-Tee launches this summer at Radley Run Country Club, with additional private clubs across the Philadelphia region to follow.

How do I book?

Through Mobili-Tee’s online scheduling platform - quick signup, available 24/7. Members pay directly by credit card, debit card, or HSA/FSA. No club account billing, no extra logins.

Can I use HSA or FSA dollars?

Yes. Mobili-Tee partners with Truemed so eligible members can apply pre-tax HSA/FSA dollars to their sessions - typically a 30% savings versus paying after tax. The qualification process takes about two minutes and is fully IRS-compliant via a Letter of Medical Necessity.

Do I need to be a member of the host club?

Yes. Mobili-Tee is offered as an on-property amenity exclusively for club members and their approved guests.
