Introducing Mobili-Tee

What is Mobili-Tee?

Mobili-Tee delivers one-on-one assisted stretching at your club. A certified specialist guides you through a personalized series of stretches on a purpose-built table — releasing tension, restoring range of motion, and supporting the way you already move through your day.

Unlike a static class or a self-guided routine, assisted stretching is a hands-on practice. Your specialist applies precise, controlled pressure to lengthen the muscles and decompress the joints, working through patterns tailored to your body.

It is a quiet, focused thirty minutes that meaningfully changes how the rest of your day — and your next round — feels.

The Experience
